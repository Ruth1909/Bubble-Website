About Us

"We are parents of toddlers, tweens and teenagers. We created a platform for busy parents to help them plan and coordinate their child's activities: never forget another basketball practice or a birthday invite. We wanted to create a tool that will make planning or finding an activity a breeze, so parents can focus on what really matters Now you should be able to easily find ways to engage your kids indoors and outdoors and easily find indoor activities for your kids.

I first came to the UK when i was a teenager, so i understand how busy parents can get from experience of growing up. Now that i have my own kids i want to create time for them. A small group of parents, I had to plan play time for my kids, something that my mum never needed to do.

As our lives got busier, planning these activities got more complicated. Long text chains. Confusion about activities. Diverging interests. The playgrounds might be empty, but the kids aren’t gone. They won't show it, but they’re bored.

I made this app to take away the pain of planning activities to keep your kids engaged. I want to remove any hesitation you might have in reaching out to friends. I hope you’ll also find new friends for your kids (and yourself) who share your interests and hobbies. This way, I hope your kids have a safe open-playground experience. Together, we’re better."

Founder

Ruth
