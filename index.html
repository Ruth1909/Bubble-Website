No more boredom for your kids - Bubbles Network matches your kid with an activity or a buddy based on his/her interests and age. We have categories like chess, painting, language clubs and a lot more!

We actively listen to parents within our communities, providing advice, support and signposting towards more appropriate specialist services. Our work with families is delivered through a series of engaging programmes that promote fun learning, strengthening of family bonds and bringing people together whilst also tackling stigmas, gender stereotyping and social isolation.

Join us online @ Bubbles.

Parent Network makes planning a breeze whether organizing a soccer practice, scheduling a meetup, or having a virtual playdate with a friend. ​ The platform allows to schedule a virtual or an in person get-together. Parents can search for their friends who are already members of the Parent Network platform and add them to their existing friend circle there. ​ It is the go to place for fun activities for kids indoors or outdoors.
